About us

Provider of information we ought to know, to become a more responsible, happier and a healthier citizen. News that help us to lead a higher quality of life and that which also improves the resilience of our city neighborhood s. AXE News communicates individual and policy level best practices towards SDGs/Agenda 2030 of UN. Triggering discussion s and debates on issues of public policy and politics of change is the vision of AXE News.
